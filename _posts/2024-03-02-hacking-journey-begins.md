## The Beginning

I'm not your typical cybersecurity professional. You can call me the "0certified kiddo" – no fancy papers, no official badges. But here's the thing: the digital world doesn't care about certificates. It cares about skills, determination, and the ability to think outside the box.

## Why I Do This

I'm not here to flex. I'm here to:
- Learn through experimentation
- Share my discoveries (and mistakes)
- Build a community of like-minded hackers
- Make the digital world a bit safer

## Current Battlegrounds

### API Hacking
This is where I spend most of my time. APIs are like the nervous system of modern applications, and finding their weak points is an art form. I focus on:
- Authentication vulnerabilities
- Authorization bypasses
- Data exposure issues

### Malware Analysis
Understanding how malware works is crucial for both offense and defense. I study:
- Evasion techniques
- Payload delivery methods
- Defense mechanisms

### Reverse Engineering
This is where the real magic happens. Taking apart binaries, understanding assembly, and figuring out how programs tick. It's like solving a puzzle where every piece fights back.
