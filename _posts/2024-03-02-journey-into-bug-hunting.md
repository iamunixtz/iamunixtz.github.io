# My Journey Into Bug Hunting

Hey digital explorers! Today, I want to share my journey into the fascinating world of bug hunting. As a young hacker from Africa, my path might be a bit different from what you typically hear about.

## The Beginning

I'm not your typical certified cybersecurity professional. I'm what you might call a "0certified kiddo" – no fancy papers, no official badges. But don't let that fool you. My passion for finding vulnerabilities and understanding system security runs deep.

## What Drives Me

Bug hunting isn't just about finding flaws – it's about understanding systems at their core. Every vulnerability is a story about how systems work, how they fail, and how they can be made better. This understanding is what drives me to keep learning and exploring.

## My Focus Areas

### API Hacking
This is where I spend a lot of my time. APIs are like the nervous system of modern applications, and finding vulnerabilities here can reveal critical security issues. I focus on:
- Authentication bypasses
- Object-level authorization flaws
- Data exposure vulnerabilities

### Malware Analysis
Understanding malware helps me think like both an attacker and a defender. It's fascinating to see how malicious code works and how it tries to evade detection. This knowledge is crucial for:
- Understanding attack vectors
- Developing better defense strategies
- Learning about system internals

## The Learning Never Stops

I'm constantly working on improving my skills in:
- C programming for low-level understanding
- Python for quick exploit development
- Assembly language for deeper system knowledge

## Looking Forward

This blog will be my digital diary where I share:
- Real-world bug hunting stories
- Vulnerability breakdowns
- Learning resources and tools
- Honest reflections on the journey

Stay tuned for more posts about my adventures in the world of cybersecurity. Whether you're just starting out or you're a seasoned pro, I hope my experiences can offer some valuable insights.

Remember: In the world of security, we're all students. Every day brings new challenges and opportunities to learn.
